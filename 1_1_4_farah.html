Artist spotlight: Farah
Farah is a 19-year-old Palestinian living in Gaza. She is the sole provider for her family, which includes three nieces with a deceased father, a nephew whose father (Farah's brother) has been imprisoned, and an ailing father with hepatitis who can no longer walk. Farah is also an incredibly talented artist, and though her aspirations of going to art school have been dashed, she's using her work to create a future for her family. You can support Farah and her family by purchasing one of her illustrations on Shopify. Each order is printed in Gainesville, Florida and mailed directly via USPS. All proceeds go to Farah. You can also donate directly to Farah on her support page. Follow Farah on Instagram for more information.
